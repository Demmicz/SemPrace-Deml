John Wick is a heartwarming tale about a retired assassin who learns the true value of life, love, and a well-stocked arsenal. It's essentially a touching dog-lover's revenge fantasy turned into an ultra-violent symphony of bullets and perfectly tailored suits. The plot, much like Wick's enemies, is shot full of holes, but hey, who needs story when Keanu Reeves is reloading in slow motion? It's like a yoga class for gun enthusiasts: calm, methodical, and surprisingly therapeutic to watch.
